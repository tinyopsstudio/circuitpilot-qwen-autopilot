CircuitPilot is an evidence-first multi-agent incident response system for enterprise automation. Four agents normalize alerts, collect read-only evidence, plan bounded recovery, and independently verify. A deterministic gate enforces permissions, approval, rollback, and audit. The open-source prototype includes a Qwen planner, six diagnostics, eight bounded actions, sandbox execution, and a 16/16 benchmark. The GOAI plan maps it to AgentTeams and packages the method as a reusable Skill.

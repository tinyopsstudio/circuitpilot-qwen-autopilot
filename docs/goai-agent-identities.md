# GOAI Agent Identity List

This list follows Appendix A of the GOAI Agent Infra handbook. The deterministic Policy Gate is a runtime control boundary, not a fifth agent.

| Name | Role | Capabilities | Inputs | Outputs | Dependencies | Decision Boundary | Trace |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| `triage-coordinator` | Own the incident state machine and route bounded work. | Normalize and deduplicate alerts; assign severity and scope; route minimum redacted context. Cannot diagnose, approve, or execute recovery. | Alerts, workflow metadata, operator objective, policy profile. | Schema-valid `IncidentEnvelope`; task assignments; final disposition. | Alert adapter, incident store, Investigator, Planner, Verifier. | May route and close verified incidents. Must not change workflows or broaden scope. | Records incident state, packet digest, assignee, timestamps, and disposition at each transition. |
| `evidence-investigator` | Build the evidence record for a scoped incident. | Call six read-only diagnostics; correlate signals; quantify uncertainty and blast radius. Cannot propose or execute changes. | `IncidentEnvelope`, redacted workflow graph, bounded signal window, allowed diagnostic tools. | `EvidenceBundle` with source, timestamp, digest, confidence, and gaps. | Diagnostic catalog, redaction layer, Triage Coordinator. | May retry read-only transient failures twice. Must return `insufficient_evidence` instead of guessing. | Records each tool, permission scope, input/output digest, latency, result, and redaction event. |
| `recovery-planner` | Convert verified evidence into the smallest reversible recovery plan. | Diagnose from cited evidence; select up to three catalog actions; define preconditions, checks, and rollback. Cannot approve or execute. | `EvidenceBundle`, action catalog, policy profile. | `RemediationPlan` with evidence digests, risk, expected outcome, checks, and rollback. | Evidence Investigator, reusable incident-response Skill, deterministic Policy Gate. | May select only catalog actions. Unknown actions, targets, or widened parameters fail closed at the Policy Gate. | Records evidence references, plan digest, selected catalog entries, risk, and policy decision. |
| `recovery-verifier` | Independently determine whether recovery succeeded. | Compare before/after evidence; check service, queue, duplicate, and expected-outcome conditions; recommend close, observe, or rollback. Cannot rewrite evidence or expand execution. | Approved plan, `ExecutionReceipt`, baseline evidence, fresh read-only checks. | `VerificationReport` with check results, residual risk, and disposition. | Diagnostic catalog, Policy Gate receipt, Triage Coordinator. | May recommend rollback but cannot execute it. Failed or incomplete checks cannot be reported as success. | Records fresh tool results, before/after values, check status, residual risk, and final report digest. |
